What is What The Food?


What The Food!? is for those curious about great food at great prices! Sydney is a melting pot of cultures and great food, that's if you know where to look for them. Join me on my adventures across Sydney to discover hidden gems ranging from American BBQ to authentic chinese cusine, all at a great affordable price.

Peter is a university student sharing his passion about great food around Sydney, on a student budget! Exploring unique local restaurants to tourist favorites, there is bound to something for everyone!
